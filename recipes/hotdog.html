Hot Dog

Description

An easy to make meal for anytime of day!

Hot dogs are extremely versitile with many toppings for you to add for your likning.

Ingredients

  • 4 good quality beef hot dogs
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Add hot dogs to cold salted water and bring them to a boil.
  2. Turn the stove off and keep the dogs in the hot water.
  3. Add olive oil to a skillet and split open the boiled hot dogs and then add them too.
  4. Cook over medium heat until brown on both sides.
