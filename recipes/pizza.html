Pizza

Description

A delicious made at home pepperoni pizza. Way better than what you can buy from a pizza shop!

It has tons of pepperoni, a nice crust and great flavor!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of pizza dough
  • 1/2 pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 oz of mozzarella cheese
  • a can of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black peppper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Add the sauce and toppings
  4. Cook the pizza
  5. Slice and serve
